Our Ministry Calling

We are delighted to welcome you to Hannah Yawson Ministry (HYM), a Christian NGO. We are the hands and feet of Christ, bringing hope, healing, and renewal to all we encounter.

Our mission is rooted in Jesus' call to serve the vulnerable and marginalized with love, dignity, and hope.

Founded by Rev. Hannah Yawson, our ministry has grown from humble beginnings to become a beacon of hope in our community.

- Rev. Hannah Yawson
